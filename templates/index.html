Our Environmental Commitment

At Clear Pani, we believe that providing pure water shouldn't come at the expense of our planet. That's why we've implemented comprehensive sustainability initiatives across our operations.

  • Eco-Friendly Bottles: Our bottles are made from 100% recycled PET plastic, reducing our carbon footprint by 30% compared to traditional bottles.
  • Zero-Waste Manufacturing: Our facilities operate on a closed-loop water system, ensuring no water is wasted during the purification process.
  • Renewable Energy: 40% of our energy needs are met through solar power, with a goal to reach 100% by 2025.
  • Recycling Program: We operate bottle collection centers across all our retail locations to ensure proper recycling of our products.
